# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
- Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts
- Keep your profiles private and review privacy settings regularly
- Think before posting any content online
- Verify information before sharing it with others
- Be respectful and maintain a positive online presence

## Don'ts
- Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or OTP
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or negative interactions
- Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people
