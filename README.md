## Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the objective was to understand and demonstrate essential digital skills, including online safety, professional communication, and the use of digital platforms.

The project consists of five tasks that focus on different aspects of digital literacy, ranging from creating awareness content to understanding cybercrime and responsible online behavior.

---

## Tasks Summary

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
Created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

### Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build an online presence.

### Task 3: Platforms Exploration
Completed a beginner-level coding task on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to spread awareness about digital literacy.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette
Drafted professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media usage.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Prepared a case study on UPI payment fraud and created a prevention checklist to promote safe online practices.
