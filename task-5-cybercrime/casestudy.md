# Case Study: UPI Payment Fraud

UPI payment fraud is a common cybercrime in India. It mostly affects students and young adults.

In a case a college student gets a call from someone who says they are a bank official or customer support person. This person tells the student that there is a problem with their bank account or that they can get a cashback reward.

The student is then asked to approve a payment or scan a QR code sent through a messaging app. The student thinks this is a request and enters their UPI PIN thinking they will get money.. Instead the money is taken from their account.

Sometimes scammers send links that take users to fake websites. These websites are designed to steal banking information. Students are often targeted because they use payment apps a lot and might not know about these scams.

UPI payment fraud can cause loss, stress and misuse of personal data. This type of cybercrime shows why it is important to be aware of risks and be careful online. UPI payment fraud is an issue that needs attention.
