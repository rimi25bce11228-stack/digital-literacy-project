# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Only use official and trusted apps for online transactions
- Do not scan QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history

## For Financial/UPI Safety
- Remember: entering your UPI PIN always means money is being sent, not received
- Double-check payment requests before approving them
